## Project – Adidas Sales Database
 Project Overview
This project uses the Adidas Sales Dataset (available on Kaggle) to analyze retail performance across stores, regions, and product categories. The goal is to extract actionable insights through Excel-based data cleaning, visualization, and dashboarding to optimize sales strategy, inventory planning, and regional targeting.
________________________________________
 Dataset Description 

The dataset contains transactional and store-level data, including:

•	Product Details: Product, Price per Unit

•	Retailer Informatio :  Retailer name, Retailer ID, Region, State,City.

•	Sales Metrics: Units Sold, Total Sales, Operating Profit, Operating Margin

•	Temporal Data: Date of Sale, Month, Year

________________________________________

 Business Objectives
 
•	Identify top-performing regions, stores, and product categories

•	Evaluate sales trends across time periods and regions

•	Analyze profitability drivers and cost structures

•	Discover seasonal patterns and inventory gaps

•	Create an interactive Excel dashboard for sales and strategy teams

________________________________________

 Key Business Questions:
 
•	Which months recorded the highest and lowest sales volumes?

•	Which retailer generated the highest total sales and operating profit ?

•	Which states or regions contribute most to overall sales volume ?

•	Which sales method (Online, In-Store, Other) dominates overall revenue ?

________________________________________

 Tools & Techniques
 
•	Microsoft Excel

o	Data Cleaning using Power Query

o	Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts

o	Conditional Formatting

o	Formulas (e.g., COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIFS, IFERROR, VLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH)

o	Dynamic Dashboards using Slicers & Timelines

o	KPI Cards & Custom Charting

________________________________________

 Deliverables
 
•	Cleaned Dataset: Nulls removed, formats standardized, derived columns 

•	Interactive Excel Dashboard: Includes slicers for Region, Store, Product Category, and Month; KPI cards for sales and profit; trend charts and category breakdowns

________________________________________

Insights (Sample Findings)

•	Top Region: Central region led in both sales and profit

•	Product Category: Footwear had the highest profit margin; Apparel had the highest volume

•	Seasonality: Sales peaked in March and November, aligning with promotional campaigns
